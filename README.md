# ⚽ worldcup2026-predictor - Create your custom tournament bracket now

[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Predictor-Blue.svg)](https://github.com/ZularifNation/worldcup2026-predictor)

This application lets you build your own FIFA World Cup 2026 tournament bracket. You decide the winners of every match from the group stages to the final. You also pick the Golden Boot winner, the MVP, and the best goalkeeper. Once your bracket is complete, you can save your predictions as a high-quality image file. The interface supports both English and Arabic.

## 🛠️ How to Use the Predictor

The application provides a simple layout to manage your tournament choices.

- Tournament Setup: Start by clicking the bracket view. You will see the full list of qualified teams. Click on a team flag or name to advance them through the brackets.
- Prediction Tools: Use the sidebar to select your individual award winners for the Golden Boot, MVP, and the best goalkeeper. 
- Language Settings: Click the language toggle button at the top right of the screen to switch between English and Arabic.
- Saving Your Work: When your bracket is full, click the "Export as PNG" button. The application creates an image of your tournament predictions and saves it directly to your computer.

## 📋 Common Questions

Can I change my predictions?
Yes. You can click any match result to clear it and choose a different team. The bracket updates automatically.

Do I need an internet connection?
Once you download and install the software, you can use it without an active internet connection.

What happens if I make a mistake?
Use the reset button to clear your bracket and start again.

Does the software track my data?
This project is open-source. It does not collect or store your personal information. Your predictions stay on your own computer.

## 📝 Performance Tips

If the application runs slowly, close other programs on your computer. Make sure you use a modern web browser to view the interface parts if you choose to run it through a browser-based environment. Check that your graphics drivers are up to date if you experience issues with the image export feature.
